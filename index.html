About us

Ebunoluwa Memorial Special High School Ibadan is a private school committed to providing quality education in a safe, nurturing, and disciplined environment. We are dedicated to developing well-rounded students who excel academically, demonstrate strong moral values, and become responsible members of society. At Ebunoluwa Memorial Special High School, we believe that every child has unique potential. Through dedicated teaching, character development, and a supportive learning atmosphere, we equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to succeed in an ever-changing world. Our focus is not only on academic excellence but also on fostering leadership, integrity, creativity, and lifelong learning.

Our mission

Shaping minds with excellence, purpose and care.

We strive to provide a nurturing environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, creativity, cultural awareness and a lifelong love of learning, while preparing students to become responsible leaders in their communities.
